HOW TO COLLECT SHAMAN

← BACK TO PORTAL
Essential Machines
Brewing Stand 100% Guaranteed
Location: Villages (Cleric's Tower), Igloo Basements, or End Ships.
How to Craft: 1 Blaze Rod + 3 Cobblestones (using Crafting Table).
Pro Tip: It is the heart of brewing. Without this, you cannot mix any potions.
Primary Ingredients
Blaze Rod 50% Drop Rate
Location: Nether Fortress.
How to get: Kill Blazes near their spawners.
Pro Tip: Use a Looting III sword to increase drop chance to 100% and get up to 4 rods.
Nether Wart Common Loot
Location: Nether Fortresses & Bastion Remnants.
How to get: Found growing on Soul Sand near fortress staircases.
Pro Tip: Always bring Soul Sand back home to start your own Nether Wart farm in the Overworld.
Rare Mob Drops
Ghast Tear 33% Drop Rate
Location: Nether (Skies).
How to get: Kill Ghasts while they are flying above solid ground.
Pro Tip: Be careful! If the tear falls into lava, it will burn and disappear forever.
Phantom Membrane 50% Drop Rate
Location: Overworld (Night Time).
How to get: Phantoms appear if you haven't slept for 3 days. Kill them.
Pro Tip: Used for Slow Falling potions, which are essential for fighting the Ender Dragon.
Pufferfish 13% Fishing Chance
Location: Warm Oceans.
How to get: Use a Fishing Rod or catch them directly with a Water Bucket.
Pro Tip: Required for Water Breathing potions. Don't eat it, it's poisonous!
